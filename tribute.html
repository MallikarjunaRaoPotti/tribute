Tribute to Ritesh Agarwal

Indian entrepreneur and the founder and CEO of OYO Rooms

Early Life

Ritesh Agarwal is the founder and CEO of OYO Rooms, a hospitality platform that offers standardized and affordable accommodations worldwide. He was born on 16 November 1993 in Bissam Cuttack, Odisha, India. He grew up in Titilagarh, where his family ran a small shop. He completed his schooling at Sacred Heart School and St. Johns Senior Secondary School. He moved to Delhi in 2011 for college, but dropped out later to pursue his entrepreneurial dreams. He started his first venture, Oravel Stays, in 2012, which was inspired by Airbnb. In 2013, he rebranded it as OYO Rooms, with a vision to provide quality and affordable lodging to travelers. /p>

Achievements and Contributions

  • Thiel Fellowship
  • Business World Young Entrepreneur Award

Legacy

Ritesh Agarwal is a remarkable example of a young and successful entrepreneur who has revolutionized the hospitality industry with his innovative and disruptive vision. His work and leadership have influenced and inspired others in many ways.
